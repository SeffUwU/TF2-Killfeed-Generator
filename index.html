HOW TO USE:

First select one of the kill icons, shown above.

Then select the name of both killers and victims.

Then using the options menu, customize your kill feed message.

Under the options menu, choose to either 'kill!' or 'dominate!' to render the message, and press 'save?' to download the result on your device.

You can also use 'CUSTOM!' to render the message with custom text after the kill icon.
