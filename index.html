HOW TO USE:

First select one of the killicons, shown above.

Then select the name of both killers and victims (or not).

Then using the options menu, customize your killfeed message.

Under the options menu, choose to either 'kill!' or 'dominate!' to render the message, and 'save?' to download the result on your device.
